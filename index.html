What's Svecial?

Svecial is a Svelte librairy with social media buttons and OAuth2 buttons. Simple to use, just put the component in your code, customize it and use it!


Svecial do all the OAuth 2 support?

No, Svecial do only the render of the target button and give you a response depend of which button you use
